Radiant is a platform-independent browser-based interface for business analytics in R, based on the Shiny package.

Business analytics app added (8/16/2015)

Breaking change (6/15/2015)

If you are using Radiant for Rmarkdown documents, State files, or from R-code please be aware that (most) function arguments have changed as of version 0.2.30. The full function manual is available at http://vnijs.github.io/radiant/radiant.pdf.
